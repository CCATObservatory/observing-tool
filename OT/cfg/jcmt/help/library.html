Guide to the Library Contents

The libraries are provided to make preparing your observing program fast and easy. To open a library go to the "File ..." menu on the OT, and select the one you need. There is currently 1 library is available:

  • SCUBA

The SCUBA library contains pre-defined observations that will be useful when building SCUBA-based science programs. It is split into three parts:

General MSBs
These MSBs will form the bulk of a science program. Pre-defined observations are provided for photometry, jiggle and scan-map observations for all filter combinations. Each MSB consists of a science observation and corresponding calibration. All you need to do is copy the MSB into your science program and edit the target information and the number of integrations.
Miscellaneous observations
These are observations that are not normally part of a science program. They include observations to determine the flat field and also to determine the instrumental polarization.
Polarimetry MSBs
Polarimetry MSBs are kept separate because they are slightly more complicated. MSBs are provided for all observing modes but only for limited filter combinations (the IP values are not at all certain at 450 microns at the time of writing). Flux calibrations are included.

If you have any questions on these libraries do not hesitate to contact your support scientist for help.
